Herbs Export Services

Small Batch Export

We offer exports of small batches measured in grams up to larger total weights. Each order is handled with precision and care, ensuring product quality from farm to destination.

Cold Storage & Refrigerated Transport

Our herbs are stored in well-equipped cold rooms and transported in refrigerated trucks to preserve freshness and aroma. Optimal handling ensures premium quality upon arrival.

Airport & Delivery Arrangement

Transport from the airport is arranged in conversation with the buyer. We coordinate logistics to guarantee smooth handover and timely delivery.

Hygiene & Quality

We ensure high hygiene standards during harvesting, packing, and transport. Products are handled carefully with protective covers, preserving quality and meeting international standards.
